4 to 9 Cafe: Unity Through Adventure & Peace

4 to 9 Cafe got its name because it’s open from 4 am to 9 pm. It was started on January 18, 2020, by Mission Thapa Magar. Originally, it was both a cafe and bar, but a few years ago, we changed it to just a cafe. We have a peaceful environment where people can enjoy chess, motorbike trips, trekking, and other activities. Our goal is to promote universal brotherhood.
